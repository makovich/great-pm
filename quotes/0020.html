Let's take a hike on the coast from San Francisco to Los Angeles to visit our friends in Newport Beach. I'll whip out my map and draw our route down the coast. The line is about 400 miles long; we can walk 4 miles per hour for 10 hours per day, so we'll be there in 10 days.
